The faculty of BVN, shapes its special character. Each faculty member is both a teacher & a scholar, an expert in his or her own field. The faculty of BVN consists of adequate number of full time highly qualified teachers, who have extensive teaching experience in their respective fields. Each staff member also imbibes the BVN culture & ethics, which is the thread that binds the entire teaching fraternity of the BVN Society.

This academic community not only imparts instructions , but also engages the students in a variety of other ways, whether it is to conduct the morning assembly, bring out school publications, arrange & participate in cultural shows or just simply arrange visits. All teachers are also involved in some school activity, like house wardens, The environment council, Editorial board cultural deptt. or sports to name a few. In fact, all teachers also share membership with students on various committees, school houses and supervise individual or small group research projects(IX to XII) The opportunities for formal & informal relationships with the BVN faculty are plentiful and rewarding , resulting in lifetime, friendships as well as professional membership that shapes student'slives and career.
