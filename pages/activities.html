Activities

House system

Education, if not incorporated with co-curricular activities cannot be considered workable and complete. The school is divided into four houses with the prime objective of inculcating in the pupils a sense of collective responsibility and solidarity, a sense of team spirit and to encourage healthy competition. Each House is under the charge of a House Mistress along with House Captains and Prefects who shoulder the responsibility through monitorial system and endure a smooth and efficient functioning of the House. Inter-House competitions are organized from time to time in various fields.

Primary Wings Activities

Activities form a regular part of the primary wing schedule

  • House activities are carried out on Wednesdays, while Thursdays and Fridays are allocated to subject and class activities respectively.

  • The primary Wing Activities include Yoga, Aerobics, Judo, Karate, Taekwondo, Skating, Music, Fine arts, Crafts, Clay modelling, Rangoli and Gardening for which special instructors visit regularly to coach the students .

  • ours and Travels are organized at regular intervals with the aim of acquainting the students with their cultural heritage.

  • Special quiz programmes are organized from time to time to keep the students in touch with Mother Nature. Regular nature walks are organized so that students can enlist physical and biotic components of environment.

  • A wide range of games and sports are a part of Primary wing schedule. Inter-class and Inter-school competitions further instill many values like discipline, self control, courage and confidence among children.

  • Special time is allotted for conversation and emphasis is laid on voice modulation and correct pronunciation. Newspaper reading is encouraged.

Games & Sports

Prime importance is given to physical fitness and sports activities in order to bring about a co-ordination between body and mind.

  • The school provides opportunity for various games such as Cricket, Football, Hockey, Basketball, Volley-Ball, Boxing, Table-Tennis, Badminton, Athletics, Roller-Skating, Yoga and Aerobics, under the guidance of trained physical education teachers and NIS coaches.

  • The infrastructure provides for a Volleyball ground, a Basket-Ball Ground, a separate Multi-purpose ground for football and athletics.

  • The school also has its own Band trained by a competent Band Master.

Excursions

Educational and Adventure Tours are organized from time to time to provide first hand knowledge and training in activities like Mountaineering, Nature-Walk etc. Educational Tours comprise visits to places of historical importance and sight seeing.

Summer Camps

A summer camp is organized annually during the summer vacations as an effort to motivate the students to utilize their vacations productively in enhancing their inherent talents. Hobby classes in Drawing and Painting, Vocal and Instrumental music, Yoga and Aerobics, Classical and Western Dancing are regular features of the Summer Camp. Inter-School Competitions Talent has always been recognized, encouraged and nurtured at Blue Bells. Students are provided wide exposure to exhibit and enhance their inherent talents through participation in various inter-school competitions. The school is well recognized for its performance in competitions at various levels.

workshop and seminar

Workshops and Seminars are organized on a regular basis for both teachers and students for enhancing their knowledge and perspective. Workshops by leading publishers providing illustrations for the course books by their authors are a regular feature. Workshops are also organized on moral & social issues by eminent experts in the field. The school has an agreement with the Management Development Institute, which conducts regular workshops for the teaching as well as non-teaching staff.
